Privacy Policy for InstinctShell

Last Updated: January 22, 2025

Important: InstinctShell is a VPN subscription management application. We do not provide VPN services directly, but help you manage your existing VPN subscriptions and server configurations.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains locally on your device.

The following information is stored only on your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third parties:

  • VLESS/VMESS subscription links and configuration data
  • Server configurations and connection parameters
  • Application preferences and settings
  • Connection logs and usage statistics (if enabled in settings)
  • Ping measurement data for server performance optimization
  • Last connected server information for Quick Connect feature

2. How We Use Your Information

The Application processes subscription data and server configurations solely for the purpose of:

  • Establishing secure connections to your configured servers
  • Managing and updating subscription information
  • Providing connection statistics and performance metrics
  • Measuring server ping times for performance optimization
  • Enhancing user experience within the application
  • Remembering your last connected server for Quick Connect functionality

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with any third parties. InstinctShell interacts with the following services:

  • Your Subscription Providers: The application fetches configuration data from the subscription links you provide. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.
  • Google Play Services: Used for application distribution and update mechanisms only.
  • Android System Services: Required for VPN functionality, network access, and device management.

Note: When you connect to VPN servers through this application, your internet traffic will be routed through the servers you configure. The privacy and security of your data during VPN connections depends on your chosen VPN service providers.

4. Permissions and Their Purposes

The Application requires the following permissions for proper functionality:

  • INTERNET: Required to connect to VPN servers and fetch subscription updates
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check network availability and optimize connections
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required to maintain active VPN connections in the background
  • WAKE_LOCK: Prevents device from sleeping during active connections
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to show connection status and allow quick disconnect from notifications
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Used to import subscription files from your device
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Used to export configuration files for backup purposes

5. Data Security and Storage

All sensitive data (subscription links, server configurations) is stored locally using Android's secure storage mechanisms:

  • Data is encrypted using Android's built-in encryption
  • No data is transmitted to external servers except to destinations you explicitly configure
  • All network communications use secure protocols (HTTPS, TLS)
  • VPN connections use industry-standard encryption methods

6. Children's Privacy

InstinctShell is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. However, when you connect to VPN servers, your data may be routed through servers in different countries based on your server selection.

8. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • You can add, modify, or remove subscription links at any time
  • All stored data can be completely erased by clearing the application data or uninstalling the application
  • You can export/import your configuration for backup purposes
  • You can disable ping measurement and other optional features in settings
  • You can clear connection logs and statistics at any time

9. Data Retention

We do not retain any data on our servers. All data is stored locally on your device and will be retained until you:

  • Delete the application
  • Clear application data through device settings
  • Manually remove specific subscriptions or configurations

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Including a notice in the application update

11. Compliance and Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
